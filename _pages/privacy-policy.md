# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 23, 2026

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
We may collect limited personal information when you make in-app purchases or contact us, including:

- **Email Address:** Collected by our subscription and analytics provider (Adapty) for purchase and subscription management  
- **Purchase Information:** Transaction history, subscription status, and product identifiers  

We do not collect your name, address, or other identifying information.

---

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device
All travel and family-related data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers, including:

- **Family Member Data:** Names, ages, relationships, passport expiration dates  
- **Trip Information:** Destinations, dates, travel details  
- **Packing Lists:** Items, quantities, and categories  
- **Documents:** Photos, PDFs, and travel documents you upload  
- **Preferences:** App settings and customization choices  

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### Subscription & Payment Processing
We use Adapty to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. Adapty may process:

- Subscription status  
- Purchase history  
- Email address (if provided)  

This information is used solely to provide paid features and customer support.

---

### Local Processing Only
All travel planning, packing lists, and document storage occur entirely on your device. We do not upload this data to any servers.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

- **Adapty** – for subscription management and purchase analytics  

Adapty may collect and process personal information according to their privacy policy. You can review Adapty’s privacy policy here:  
https://adapty.io/privacy-policy/

We do not sell or share your personal data for advertising purposes.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

### Local Data
- All travel and family data is stored locally on your device  
- We do not operate servers that store this personal information  

### Payment Data
- Payment processing is handled by Google Play and Adapty  
- We do not store credit card or payment details  

---

## 5. Your Rights & Choices

### Data Control
- You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app  
- You can manage or cancel subscriptions through Google Play  
- You can request access to or deletion of Adapty-related data by contacting us  

---

## 6. Data Deletion Requests

To delete locally stored data, uninstall the app from your device.

To request deletion of subscription-related data (such as email or purchase records), contact us at:

**Email:** help@margostudios.com  

We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended to collect personal information from children.  
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

---

## 8. International Data Transfers

Adapty and Google Play may process data on servers located in different countries.  
We do not store or transfer personal data on our own servers.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
